The "returner" set traces three return-event categories: low-energy Earth co-orbital trajectories that resonantly re-encounter on year-class periods; weakly-bound Earth-Sun-Moon manifold orbits like J002E3 with multi-decade return cycles; and engineered disposal trajectories whose orbital geometry was chosen for rapid solar-system clearance but unintentionally keeps the stage Earth-resonant.

For Cert-2 Vulcan Centaur the "return" is three close approaches over two years (2026-Nov-10, 2027-Dec-10, 2028-Dec-23), not a single capture event.

FutureWarn entry 2032-Jun-01 flags expected return Jul 2032.
