Formula

$$\mathrm{SNR} = \frac{S_{\star}}{\sqrt{S_{\star} + S_{\mathrm{sky}} + S_{\mathrm{dark}} + S_{\mathrm{RN}}}}$$ $$S_{\star} = 10^{-0.4\,m}\;\cdot F_0 \cdot A \cdot t \cdot Q_{\mathrm{eff}}$$ $$Q_{\mathrm{eff}} = \eta_{\mathrm{QE}}\;\cdot\;T_{\mathrm{optics}}\;\cdot\;T_{\mathrm{atm}}$$ $$T_{\mathrm{atm}} = 10^{-0.4\,k\,X}\,,\qquad X = \sec z = \frac{1}{\cos(90°-\mathrm{elev})}$$ $$S_{\mathrm{sky}} = 10^{-0.4\,\mu_{\mathrm{sky}}}\;\cdot F_0 \cdot \Omega_{\mathrm{PSF}} \cdot A \cdot t \cdot Q_{\mathrm{eff}}$$ $$S_{\mathrm{dark}} = I_{\mathrm{dark}}\;\cdot\;t\;\cdot\;N_{\mathrm{pix}}\,,\qquad S_{\mathrm{RN}} = \sigma_{\mathrm{read}}^{2}\;\cdot\;N_{\mathrm{frames}}\;\cdot\;N_{\mathrm{pix}}$$ $$N_{\mathrm{pix}} = \frac{\pi}{4}\!\left(\frac{\theta_{\mathrm{FWHM}}}{\Delta\theta}\right)^{2}\!,\qquad \Delta\theta = \frac{d_{\mathrm{pix}}}{D\cdot f/N}\;\cdot\;\frac{648000}{\pi}\;\;[\mathrm{arcsec/pix}]$$
